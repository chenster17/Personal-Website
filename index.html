A Little About Myself:

I am a second year computer science student at the University of Toronto. Ever since I was a child and I had learned that my favourite games were made with only a couple lines of english words, I discovered my interest in computer science. When I was fourteen, I learned my first language Python, then eventually Java. When I was sixteen, I built my first computer from scratch, a computer I still use to this day. At the University of Toronto, I'm involved with clubs such as Computer Science Club, Canadian Asian Student Society, Chinese Undergraduates Association at U of T, and Super Smash Brother Club. In my free time, I enjoy playing basketball, playing badminton, playing ping pong, cooking, and playing video games (mostly open world games like Dark Souls).
